## ⏰ 时态 (Tenses)

### 1. 一般现在时

**构成**: 主语 + 动词原形/动词-s

**用法**:
- 经常性动作: I **go** to school every day.
- 客观事实: The earth **is** round.
- 习惯性动作: She **works** hard.

**时间标志词**:
- every day / week / month
- always / usually / often
- sometimes / never

**第三人称单数**:
| 原形 | 第三人称单数 |
|------|--------------|
| go | goes |
| do | does |
| have | has |
| watch | watches |
| study | studies |

### 2. 现在进行时

**构成**: 主语 + am/is/are + 动词-ing

**用法**:
- 正在进行的动作: I **am reading** now.
- 将来计划: I **am meeting** John tomorrow.
- 临时情况: The store **is closing** down.

**动词-ing变化规则**:
| 规则 | 原形 | -ing形式 |
|------|------|----------|
| 一般加-ing | work | working |
| 去e加-ing | make | making |
| 重读闭音节双写 | run | running |
| ie变y加-ing | lie | lying |

### 3. 一般过去时

**构成**: 主语 + 动词过去式

**用法**:
- 过去的动作: I **went** to Beijing yesterday.
- 过去的状态: She **was** happy then.

**规则动词过去式**:
| 规则 | 原形 | 过去式 |
|------|------|--------|
| 一般加-ed | work | worked |
| 去e加-ed | live | lived |
| y变i加-ed | study | studied |
| 双写加-ed | stop | stopped |

**不规则动词** (常见):
| 原形 | 过去式 | 过去分词 |
|------|--------|----------|
| go | went | gone |
| come | came | come |
| see | saw | seen |
| do | did | done |
| make | made | made |
| take | took | taken |
| get | got | got |
| have | had | had |
| eat | ate | eaten |
| drink | drank | drunk |
| sleep | slept | slept |
| speak | spoke | spoken |
| tell | told | told |
| say | said | said |

### 4. 过去进行时

**构成**: 主语 + was/were + 动词-ing

**用法**:
- 过去正在进行的动作: I **was reading** when you called.
- 背景描述: The sun **was shining**.

**时间标志词**:
- when / while
- at that time
- yesterday at 5pm

### 5. 现在完成时

**构成**: 主语 + have/has + 过去分词

**用法**:
- 过去动作对现在的影响: I **have lost** my keys. (现在开不了门)
- 从过去持续到现在: I **have lived** here for 5 years.
- 经历: I **have visited** Paris twice.

**时间标志词**:
- already / yet / just
- for / since
- ever / never
- recently / lately
- so far / up to now

### 6. 过去完成时

**构成**: 主语 + had + 过去分词

**用法**:
- 过去的过去: When I arrived, the movie **had started**.
- 希望/打算: I **had hoped** to come.

### 7. 一般将来时

**构成**:
- will + 动词原形: I **will come** tomorrow.
- be going to + 动词原形: I **am going to study** tonight.

**区别**:
- **will**: 临时决定、预测、承诺
- **be going to**: 计划、迹象

### 8. 将来进行时

**构成**: 主语 + will be + 动词-ing

**用法**:
- 将来正在进行的动作: This time tomorrow, I **will be flying** to Tokyo.
- 预计发生: I **will be working** late tonight.

## 🔄 语态 (Voice)

### 1. 主动语态 vs 被动语态

**主动语态**: 主语 + 动词 + 宾语
- The dog **bit** the man.

**被动语态**: 主语 + be + 过去分词
- The man **was bitten** by the dog.

### 2. 被动语态变化规则

| 时态 | 主动 | 被动 |
|------|------|------|
| 一般现在 | I make it. | It **is made** by me. |
| 一般过去 | I made it. | It **was made** by me. |
| 现在进行 | I **am making** it. | It **is being made**. |
| 过去进行 | I **was making** it. | It **was being made**. |
| 现在完成 | I **have made** it. | It **has been made**. |
| 过去完成 | I **had made** it. | It **had been made**. |
| 一般将来 | I **will make** it. | It **will be made**. |

### 3. 被动语态用法

- 不知道动作执行者: My phone **was stolen**.
- 强调动作接受者: The movie **was directed** by Zhang Yimou.
- 正式/客观: It **is reported** that...
- 常用被动动词: believe, know, say, report, think, consider

## 📝 从句 (Clauses)

### 1. 名词性从句

**主语从句**:
- **That** he failed surprised us.
- **What** he said is true.

**宾语从句**:
- I know **that** you are right.
- Tell me **what** you need.

**表语从句**:
- The problem is **that** we have no money.
- The question is **whether** he will come.

### 2. 定语从句

**关系代词**:
| 人 | 物 |
|----|----|
| who | which / that |
| whom | that |
| whose | whose |

**例子**:
- The man **who** came here is my friend. (人，主语)
- The book **that** I read is interesting. (物，主语/宾语)
- This is the girl **whose** father is a doctor. (所有格)

**限定性 vs 非限定性**:
- 限定性: The book **that** you gave me is good. (不能省略)
- 非限定性: My book, **which** you borrowed, is good. (可省略)

### 3. 状语从句

**时间从句**:
- when / while / as (当...时)
- before / after (之前/之后)
- as soon as / once (一...就...)
- until / till (直到)
- since / now that (自从/既然)

**原因从句**:
- because (因为)
- since (既然)
- as (由于)
- now that (既然)

**条件从句**:
- if (如果)
- unless (除非)
- as long as (只要)
- provided that (假如)

**让步从句**:
- although / though (虽然)
- even if / even though (即使)
- however / whatever (无论)
- no matter what/where/how (无论)

**目的从句**:
- so that / in order that (为了)

**结果从句**:
- so...that (如此...以至于)
- such...that (如此...以至于)

## 🎯 非谓语动词 (Non-finite Verbs)

### 1. 动词不定式 (to + v.)

**用法**:
- 作主语: **To learn** English is important.
- 作宾语: I want **to go** home.
- 作定语: I have a lot of homework **to do**.
- 作状语: I came here **to see** you.
- 作补语: I want you **to help** me.

**不带to的情况**:
- let / make / have sb do sth
- see / hear / watch sb do sth

### 2. 动名词 (v.-ing)

**用法**:
- 作主语: **Learning** English is fun.
- 作宾语: I enjoy **reading**.
- 作定语: **Swimming** pool

**动名词 vs 现在分词**:
- 动名词具有名词性质: **Swimming** is good. (游泳是好的)
- 现在分词具有形容词性质: The **swimming** boy is him. (正在游泳的男孩)

### 3. 现在分词 (v.-ing)

**用法**:
- 作定语: The **running** water is clear.
- 作表语: The story is **interesting**.
- 作宾补: I saw him **running**.
- 作状语: **Being** tired, I went to bed early.

**现在分词与过去分词区别**:
- 现在分词: 主动、进行
- 过去分词: 被动、完成

## ✅ 主谓一致 (Subject-Verb Agreement)

### 1. 语法一致

**单数主语**:
- One apple **is** enough.
- The news **is** good.

**复数主语**:
- Two apples **are** enough.
- They **are** students.

### 2. 意义一致

**集体名词**:
- My family **is** large. (整体)
- My family **are** watching TV. (成员)

**表示数量的词组**:
- Three hours **is** enough. (视为整体)
- Three hours **have** passed. (强调个体)

### 3. 就近一致

**either...or / neither...nor**:
- Either you **or** I **am** wrong.
- Neither the students **nor** the teacher **knows**.

**not only...but also**:
- Not only the teacher **but also** the students **are** present.

## 💭 虚拟语气 (Subjunctive Mood)

### 1. if 虚拟语气

| 时间 | 从句 | 主句 |
|------|------|------|
| 现在 | If + 主 + 过去式 | 主 + would + 动词原形 |
| 过去 | If + 主 + had + 过去分词 | 主 + would + have + 过去分词 |
| 将来 | If + 主 + were to / should + 动词原形 | 主 + would + 动词原形 |

**例子**:
- 现在: If I **were** you, I **would accept** the offer.
- 过去: If I **had studied** hard, I **would have passed**.
- 将来: If it **should rain**, I **would stay** home.

### 2. wish 虚拟

**表达愿望**:
- I wish I **were** a bird. (现在不可能)
- I wish I **had studied** harder. (过去后悔)
- I wish it **would stop** raining. (将来希望)

### 3. suggest 虚拟

**建议类动词** (suggest, advise, recommend, propose):
- suggest + (should) + 动词原形
- I suggest **that** he **should leave** now.

## 📚 常见错误

### 错误1: 主谓不一致
❌ He **go** to school every day.
✅ He **goes** to school every day.

### 错误2: 混用时态
❌ I **am** here since 2020.
✅ I **have been** here since 2020.

### 错误3: 被动语态错误
❌ The book **wrote** by him.
✅ The book **was written** by him.

### 错误4: 从句关系词错误
❌ This is the man **which** I met yesterday.
✅ This is the man **whom** I met yesterday.

### 错误5: 虚拟语气错误
❌ If I **was** rich, I **would buy** a car.
✅ If I **were** rich, I **would buy** a car.
