## 📖 常用表达

### 表达观点
- I think... (我认为...)
- In my opinion... (在我看来...)
- I believe... (我相信...)
- It seems to me that... (在我看来...)
- From my point of view... (从我的角度来看...)
- I would say... (我会说...)
- As far as I know... (据我所知...)
- Personally, I think... (我个人认为...)

### 表达同意
- I agree with you. (我同意你的看法。)
- That's a good point. (这是个很好的观点。)
- I couldn't agree more. (我完全同意。)
- Exactly! / Absolutely! (完全正确！)
- You are right. (你是对的。)
- I feel the same way. (我有同感。)

### 表达不同意
- I see what you mean, but... (我理解你的意思，但是...)
- I'm not sure about that. (我不太确定。)
- I disagree because... (我不同意，因为...)
- I have a different opinion. (我有不同的看法。)
- That may be true, but... (那可能是真的，但是...)
- I see it differently. (我的看法不同。)

### 表达原因
- The reason is that... (原因是...)
- This is because... (这是因为...)
- Due to / Owing to... (由于...)
- Thanks to... (多亏了...)
- As a result of... (作为...的结果)
- Therefore / Consequently... (因此/所以...)

### 表达结果
- As a result... (结果...)
- Therefore... (因此...)
- So... (所以...)
- That's why... (这就是为什么...)
- This leads to... (这导致...)
- Consequently... (于是...)
